Nikki Garcia Aims To Be A Cornhole Champion In Charity Pro-Am Tournament

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  225 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Nikki Garcia is ready to play cornhole for a good cause.

The latest round of the American Cornhole League’s Johnsonville SuperHole IV preliminary Pro-Am will take place at Milwaukee’s Summerfest on Friday, July 7.

The Johnsonville SuperHole IV pairs celebrities with ACL cornhole pros in the pro-am style tournament. Winners will advance to the Johnsonville SuperHole IV Championship in August, where they’ll be able to compete for a charity of their choice.

Nikki Garcia (fka Nikki Bella) will compete alongside Green Bay Packers star running back , former NFL player and viral influencer , and country music star  in the preliminary event.

Nikki Garcia is the second pro wrestling personality to compete in the Johnsonville SuperHole IV preliminary Pro-Am. Charlotte Flair also competed in the third leg of the tournament in May.

Charlotte Flair Competes At American Cornhole League’s Johnsonville SuperHole IV (Photos/Video)

The celebrity/pro pairings are as follows:

  • Aaron Jones and Yetty Irwan
  • Nikki Garcia and Eric Zocklein
  • Spice Adams and David Morse
  • Tyler Hubbard and Dayton Webber

The Johnsonville SuperHole IV matchups will be broadcast live from Summerfest on July 7 on ESPN2 at 8 p.m. ET/7 p.m. CT.

Nikki and Brie Garcia confirmed their WWE departures earlier this year. After leaving the company, the twins announced they would no longer use the Bellas name and would professionally be known as The Garcia Twins.
