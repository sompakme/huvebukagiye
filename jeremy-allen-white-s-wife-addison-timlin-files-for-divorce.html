Jeremy Allen Whites Wife Addison Timlin Files for Divorce

 Posted on July 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  480 words  |  Aldo Pusey

It’s over! Jeremy Allen White’s wife, Addison Timlin, has filed for divorce after more than three years of marriage.

The All Roads to Perla actress, 31, filed the paperwork on Thursday, May 11, in Los Angeles, according to court documents obtained by Us Weekly.

Timlin submitted a divorce petition categorized as “dissolution with minor child” on Thursday with the Stanley Mosk Courthouse. The filing falls under the “family law division” in L.A.

The estranged spouses, who tied the knot in October 2019, first met in 2008 while filming Afterschool.

The Bear star, 32, and the Feast of the Seven Fishes actress have played coy about their relationship for years — even while Timlin was pregnant with both of their children. The pair share daughter Ezer, 4, and daughter Dolores, 2.

“We made a baby and my heart is bursting at the seams,” Timlin announced in June 2018, revealing her baby bump amid her first pregnancy. “Today is my birthday but my wish already came true. This is 27 and the best is yet to come ❤️.”

The Philadelphia native shared a photo of her then-boyfriend and their firstborn child via Instagram in October 2018 after daughter Ezer made her appearance.

“Ezer Billie White 10.20.2018 Welcome to the world baby girl, it just got so much brighter,” Timlin captioned the snap.

Us exclusively confirmed in April 2019 that the Shameless alum and the Life Like actress were engaged. The pair tied the knot six months later surrounded by their loved ones — and a few famous faces.

Dakota Johnson and boyfriend Chris Martin were two of the celebs in attendance when the duo said “I do” in October 2019. (The Fifty Shades of Grey actress, 33, is Ezer’s godmother.)

White and Timlin expanded their brood in December 2020 with the addition of daughter Dolores. “She is the answer to 1000 prayers and we are in love with her,” the proud mom wrote via Instagram after giving birth.

In August 2022, White gushed over his children and revealed that his girls had helped his marriage.

“I learn a lot from them,” he told InStyle at the time. “I feel like in a lot of ways, they’re teaching me all the time how to be better: how to be better to them; how to be better to my wife, my friends, my parents, my sister. I think I’ve been ready to domesticate for a long time, and I’m so happy to be in the house all the time with them.”
