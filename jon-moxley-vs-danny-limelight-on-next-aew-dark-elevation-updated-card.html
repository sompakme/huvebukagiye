Jon Moxley vs. Danny Limelight On Next AEW Dark Elevation, Updated Card

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  503 words  |  Martina Birk

Jon Moxley will be making his way to AEW Dark Elevation for a second consecutive Monday. Mox mixed it up with Andrew Palace last week and ultimately got the win with the rear naked choke he’s been applying on opponents, but this week he’s got the ever-impressive Danny Limelight on his hands.

Limelight has figuratively and literally been a highlight on Dark, Dark Elevation and even Dynamite when he tagged with the Varsity Blonds back in January, but now he’s got the high solo task of taking on the former AEW World Heavyweight Champion. Limelight, however, remains confident in taking on his upcoming opponent.

“Mi Gente! You still sleeping on me?” Limelight Tweeted. “Stepping into the ring with one of the best in world once again this Monday on AEW
Elevation. Jon Moxley, bring it papa.”
