Jenna Dewan-Tatum has proven that she is a star in her own right- she’s not just the wife of Channing Tatum, she has star power all her own. She even has her own fanbase. This dancer/actress has been working hard in the industry proving to the world that she can handle things all on her own. She has found success through many different areas in the entertainment industry. She started off as a dancer before she made her way into the movie industry. That was where she ended up meeting Channing after all! They co-starred together in the movie Step Up, and that was the beginning of their love story. It actually wasn’t very long before they were married and started their own family.

But that wasn’t enough for Jenna- she wanted to take over the world as well, and she’s certainly well on her way. She is known to be wildly friendly and has no problem speaking her mind on social media. But on the other hand, she is also very private and protects the things that are most important to her. The reason why fans love her so much is because she is genuine and charming- never mind the fact that she’s drop-dead gorgeous. You may think that you know this beauty, but there are so many things you just don’t know. She’s not the kind of girl to stand in her husband's shadow- we're pretty sure she wants to present a shadow of her own. Check out these 15 fun facts about our favorite celeb of the moment, Jenna Dewan-Tatum.

15. She Wants To Erase Her First Drinking Experience

I can certainly relate to Jenna’s experience; I really think that we all can. I mean who really has a great experience their first time drinking? I think we all underestimate the effect of alcohol on a body that’s just not used to it. We often go a little heavier than we should and before we know it we are hanging over the porcelain God. The first time Jenna tried drinking was in high school. “My first go at drinking did not go so well. [Laughs.] I ended up getting sick in front of everybody. You know that Ginuwine song “Pony?” It was playing at the party while I was getting sick. My entire senior year, everyone sang that around me. For a year. I would like that erased from my memory.” We can probably all agree that was a humiliating experience, definitely more so than the experience I had myself as a teenager.

She wanted the world to know that it was not okay to use lizard, snake, alligator or crocodile skin for use in the fashion industry at all. She doesn’t believe that the fashion industry should be using these skins in this day and age. She’s looking for a change in the industry, and she's okay with making her opinion known.

They must have been close to some degree because she was invited to go to Neverland Ranch for a party. We are totally willing to admit that we are completely jealous about it. 12. Shooting Music Videos

You would think that shooting music videos would be glamorous and so much fun to do, but Jenna doesn’t feel that way. She has appeared in a few videos as a dancer and has attributed her success to the fact that she was in two of Janet Jackson’s videos. After she was in the videos Janet asked her to go on tour with her and that gave her even more experience. As much as Jenna liked the experience she wasn’t a big fan of shooting videos. She once stated: “Music videos are notoriously long, not fun, grueling. You are known there as a dancer, and it’s kind of sad because dancers, in a lot of ways, are under-appreciated and kind of under-respected when it comes to that, so they don’t necessarily treat you in a nice way when you do a music video.” We would always treat Jenna well, that's for sure.

11. She’s A Vegan

Being a vegan is not an easy decision for anyone. In fact, I couldn’t imagine doing it even for one day! But vegans have strong opinions about eating animals, and when it comes right down to it, they don’t see it as a big sacrifice. Jenna has always been known to have an insane body, and a lot of that is attributed to the fact that she’s a dancer. She, however, believes that it also has to do with her vegan diet- one which keeps her pretty lean. She originally planned on being a vegetarian but eventually she switched to being a vegan because she’s a strong animal rights supporter. She said, “after going vegan, I felt so much better. My skin cleared up; I had a ton of more energy and I just felt clearer in the head.” She tried to get Channing on board as well, but he only lasted a few months before giving up on it. In the meantime, the two of them are raising their daughter as a vegetarian and eventually they will allow her to make a choice for herself.

In fact, she brings these items wherever she travels. “Every hotel room, every apartment we rent, I am sage-ing. And I have crystals that I travel with. It just makes me feel better.” It probably gives her the right peace of mind so that she can stay focused as an artist. I’ve always believed that mediation is key to a positive mindset as well. Good for you Jenna!

8. Her Favorite Activity Is Riding Her Bike

There are many different ways that we all find to relax and regroup in our lives. We either choose to exercise or read, spend time with friends, or go on vacation. It all depends what floats your boat. For Jenna, it’s riding her bike, and her favorite place to do that is on Nantucket. She literally takes a bike out and goes for a ride. Her family has a house there, and it’s been in the family for many generations. She loves going there and spending the time to relax, and bike riding is one way she is able to unwind. “My family has had a house on Nantucket for many generations, and one of my favorite things to do is to ride my bike there. Considering the outgoing and charming personality that she has it’s no surprise that Jenna was one of the popular kids in school.
