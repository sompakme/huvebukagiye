SZA, Victoria Monet And More

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  19 minutes  |  4009 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The 66th Grammy Awards went down Sunday (Feb. 4) in Los Angeles, celebrating the biggest names in music and awarding countless talented artists, many of whom went home with multiple prizes.

Victoria Monét was one of them, taking home one of the Big 4 trophies (Best New Artist) as well as multiple wins in the R&B category. The night’s most nominated artist, SZA, also took home multiple awards, with both ladies becoming emotional during their acceptance speeches.

In Hip-Hop, Killer Mike dominated, winning three awards, while Lil Durk and J. Cole walked away with a trophy for their collab, “All My Life.”

Other notable winners include Coco Jones, Dave Chappelle, Miley Cyrus, and Taylor Swift, who won her fourth Album of the Year award, setting a new record.
