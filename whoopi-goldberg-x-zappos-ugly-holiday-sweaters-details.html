Whoopi Goldberg x Zappos Ugly Holiday Sweaters: Details

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  165 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

You knew ugly could be so cute? Whoopi Goldberg is once again teaming up with Zappos on a line of cheeky holiday sweaters, and this year’s 16-piece collection includes styles for the whole family. Whether you’re looking to spread a bit of holiday cheer or win that ugly sweater contest at the office, the Whoopi x Zappos collection includes sweaters for Chanukah, Christmas and New Years in both children’s and adult sizing.

Who could say no to an octopus menorah? Or turning your chest into an Advent calendar? The whole unisex collection is colorful, oversized and meant for cozying up. Other funny prints from the collab include a light-up Christmas Tree skirt poncho, reindeers kissing under the mistletoe, Mrs. Claus taking over for Santa and more.
