Joe Jonas Files For Divorce From Sophie Turner, Wants To Uphold Prenup To Protect $50 Million Fortun

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  440 words  |  Martina Birk

According to reports, Joe Jonas has filed for divorce from Sophie Turner,.

On Tuesday, singer officially filed for divorce from his wife, citing that their marriage is "irretrievably broken." The divorce petition was submitted in Miami Dade County, Florida, which is where the couple had recently sold their $15 million mansion.

In addition to declaring the marriage as irreparable, Jonas' filing emphasizes that it is in the best interests of their two children for him and Turner to share parental responsibility.

Joe has a reported fortune of $50 million, while Sophie, best known for her role in Game of Thrones, has a net worth of $12 million. Though their various assets couple complicate a divorce, reports say Joe and Sophie signed a prenuptial agreement prior to their 2019 wedding. Joe is requesting the prenup be upheld in his divorce filing.

Earlier this month, several media outlets have reported that the long-time couple, who have two daughters together, are preparing to conclude their marriage of four years. An insider disclosed to PEOPLE that Joe has taken the step of retaining a divorce attorney.

TMZ's sources confirm the same, disclosing that Joe's representatives have been in contact with at least two divorce attorneys in the Los Angeles region, seeking guidance.

The insiders additionally assert that the couple has been contending with significant issue for at least six months.

Over the past three months, it's purported that Joe has assumed the primary role in caring for their two young children, even amidst his band's busy touring schedule. At present, he serves as the primary caregiver for both children while the Jonas Brothers continue their tour across the United States.

Joe hasn’t commented on the divorce news, though he has been spotted wearing his wedding ring since the news broke, including during a recent performance in Austin, Texas.

In 2019, Joe and Sophie spontaneously married in Las Vegas and followed it up with an intimate ceremony at Chateau de Tourreau in the southern part of France a month later. Their first child, a daughter named Willa, was born in July 2020, and their second child arrived two years later. They have not disclosed their youngest baby’s name,

During an appearance on "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon" last year, Turner shared that she had a strong sense that she would marry Jonas from the very moment they first met in 2016. So far, the actress has commented on Joe’s petition to file for divorce.
