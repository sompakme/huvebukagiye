The Mission Impossible franchise is one of the most popular and highest-grossing movie franchises globally, grossing over $3.5 Billion worldwide. The franchise has remained a staple of the action movie genre for over two decades. With its thrilling stunts, high-octane chases, and jaw-dropping special effects, it's no wonder that fans eagerly await each new installment. One of the most exciting aspects of these films is the incredible array of vehicles that the characters drive.

Starting from exquisite Lamborghinis to rugged Land Rovers, a number of luxury supercars are seen in the Mission Impossible franchise spanning six movies.

2003 Lincoln Town Car Stretched Limousine - $40,000

The 2003 Lincoln Town Car Stretched Limousine is a classic choice for high-stakes missions. In Mission Impossible: III, this luxurious vehicle is used for a kidnapping scene involving Ethan Hunt’s wife. The limousine's spacious interior and elegant design is perfect for making a grand entrance. Under the hood, it boasts a 4.6-liter V8 engine, providing ample power for those high-speed getaways.

2013 Land-Rover Range Rover Series IV - $50,000

In Mission Impossible: Rogue Nation, the 2013 Land Rover Range Rover Series IV is the vehicle of choice for the Syndicate, a rogue organization that Ethan Hunt and his team must take down. This rugged SUV has a 5.0-liter V8 engine, producing 375 horsepower and 375 lb-ft of torque. The car's off-road capabilities and luxurious interior make it the perfect blend of style and substance.

Alfa Romeo 166 - $50,000

The sleek and stylish Alfa Romeo 166 appears in Mission Impossible: III, driven by one of the villains while pursuing Ethan Hunt during a high-speed chase through the streets of Italy. This Italian sedan is powered by a 3.2-liter V6 engine, generating 240 horsepower and 213 lb-ft of torque, reaching a top speed of 152 MPH, as per Screenrant. The elegant design and impressive performance make it a fitting choice for any secret agent.

Land Rover Defender - $57,875

A recurring favorite in the Mission Impossible franchise, the Land Rover Defender is a versatile off-road vehicle with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine and a top speed of 149 MPH. Its rugged design and exceptional performance made it a go-to choice for the IMF team in various installments of the series, including a memorable scene in Ghost Protocol where the vehicle was used to escape a sandstorm in Dubai.

BMW M3 - $70,000

The BMW M3, featured in Rogue Nation, is a high-performance sports car with a 3.0-liter twin-turbo inline-six engine, generating 425 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. This powerful vehicle was used in a thrilling chase scene, where Ethan Hunt expertly navigated the narrow streets of Morocco while evading his pursuers.

Porsche 911 Convertible - $100,000

The Porsche 911 Convertible, showcased in Mission Impossible: II, is a classic sports car with an impressive 3.4-liter flat-six engine capable of generating 300 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. This iconic vehicle played a pivotal role in a high-speed chase as Ethan Hunt pursued former professional thief Nyah Nordoff-Hall going around in circles.

BMW M5 - $110,000

The BMW M5, featured in Mission Impossible: Fallout, is a high-performance sedan with a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, producing 600 horsepower and capable of reaching an impressive top speed of 155 MPH as per Hot Cars. This powerful vehicle was seen in the movie's opening scene at Ramstein Air Base, where the IMF secretary, Allan Hunley, drives it away.

2005 Aston Martin V8 Vantage - $130,000

The 2005 Aston Martin V8 Vantage, which appeared in Mission Impossible: Ghost Protocol, is a luxury sports car with a 4.3-liter V8 engine, generating 380 horsepower and 302 lb-ft of torque. This elegant vehicle caught the eye of Ethan Hunt during a high-stakes mission in Dubai, showcasing its elegant design and impressive performance, as per Dubizzle. The car is currently priced at a whopping $130,000, making it one of the most expensive cars used in the Mission Impossible franchise.

BMW i8 - $170,000

The BMW i8, another star of Ghost Protocol, is a futuristic hybrid sports car with a 1.5-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine and an electric motor, producing a combined 357 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. This innovative vehicle was used in a high-speed pursuit through the streets of Mumbai to stop the villain Hendricks from facilitating the sale of a Soviet military satellite. The i8 effortlessly highlighted its cutting-edge technology and exceptional performance as Ethan Hunt and his team worked to prevent a global catastrophe.

Lamborghini Gallardo - $200,000

The $200,000 Lamborghini Gallardo is the showstopper of this list. Featured in Mission Impossible: III, this high-performance supercar is powered by a 5.0-liter V10 engine, generating an enormous 520 horsepower and a staggering 376 lb-ft of torque. Its jaw-dropping top speed of 202 MPH and striking design made it the perfect vehicle for Ethan Hunt to escape Vatican City and his fellow IMF team member Zhen. The Gallardo appeared on-screen and off-screen in MI 3, as MotorTrend reported that the makers transported the cast to the film's world premiere in Rome using 10 of these high-speed beasts.

The Mission Impossible franchise has consistently delivered thrilling action sequences and unforgettable car chases, featuring some of the world's most iconic and luxurious vehicles. All the ten cars featured in this list have played an integral role in the series' success, leaving audiences on the edge of their seats and eagerly anticipating the next installment. The franchise continues pushing the boundaries of automotive excellence as we wait for the 7th installment, Dead Reckoning, to release on 12 July 2023.
