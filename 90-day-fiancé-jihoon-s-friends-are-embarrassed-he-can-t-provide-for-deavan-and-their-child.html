90 Day Fianc: Jihoon's Friends Are Embarrassed He Cant Provide For Deavan And Their Child

 Posted on July 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  518 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In the latest episode of 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way once Deavan moves with their baby and her daughter Drascilla, Deavan and Jihoon are still having a hard time figuring out how they'll set up their new life in South Korea. Jihoon quit his job right when Deavan had just given birth to their child. Now that it's just Deavan who's working to support the family, she's worried that things are not going to work once she makes the move.

Deavan's mother is also worried about her plans that she's coming to Seoul with her just to see how their new life is going to look like there. Given the high standards of Deavan's mom, Jihoon is just caught in a bad position, hinting at some more drama to come between the couple.

Jihoon Lies To Deavan About Having A Job

In the previous episode of 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way, Jihoon tells Deavan on a video call that he finally got a job. Deavan promised that she'll only go back to South Korea if Jihoon finally steps up. Still, she's taking care of everything. She's even renting their apartment in Seoul because Jihoon wasn't able to take care of it.

Jihoon gives Deavan vague details about his job, saying he's making good money (well it kinda is when you have a baby to support). However, when she asks how much he's earning, he just says he doesn't know. Jihoon gives a not so sure estimate of $3000-4000, and Deavan says it's just not enough to support their whole family.

Jihoon dismisses the discussion by complaining that it's just all about money and tells Deavan to stop being grumpy. At this point, Deavan is just unsure of what's going to happen when she moves to Seoul, but she's certain that this will be the last chance she's giving him and that she'll go back home if Jihoon fails to step up.

Jihoon's Friends Say He's Making A Fool Of Korean Men

Jihoon meets his friends to talk about his family's arrival in Seoul with his mother-in-law. His friends ask him about his job, and he finally admits that all he's doing is some deliveries and that it's just a part-time gig.

His friends say they are embarrassed by Jihoon as they believe he's doing a poor job at being the man of the family and that he's crazy for lying about his job to Deavan.
