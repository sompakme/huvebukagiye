Judith Ivey Biography

Judith Ivey is an American theatre director and actress, who was born and brought up in El Paso, Texas, the United States. She is the daughter of Nathan Aldean Ivey, a college instructor, and dean and Dorothy Lee who was a teacher. She is widely recognized for her roles in her Actress career in various films like Steaming in 1983 and Hurlyburly in 1985. These films gave her credit and she won the Tony Award for Best Featured Actress in a Play for her performances in Steaming and Hurlyburly.
