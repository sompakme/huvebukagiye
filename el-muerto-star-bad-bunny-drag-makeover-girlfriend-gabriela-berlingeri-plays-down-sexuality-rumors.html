Bad Bunny is completely straight and is not a gay man. Who is he romantically involved with?

Bad Bunny, a rapper, is heading on to the acting field after landing the Marvel role.

Born Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, this Puerto Rican musical artist has over two dozen of song credits to his name. To name a few are Yonaguni, Diles, Dakiti, Booker T, Amorfoda, etc.

Recently, Bad Bunny caught the public sight after taking on a lead in the Sony Marvel movie El Muerto and will play a spiderman.

Debunked: Is Bad Bunny Gay? No, He Is Certainly Not

Though Bad Bunny is not gay, he has been long considered an LGBTQ icon and ally as per The Guardian.

Given his activism for the Latin queer community, he received this title as an iconic figure in this community. What’s more, in 2010, he was complimented for raising his voice against the murder of trans women during a live performance on US TV in Puerto Rico.

Likewise, he even showed his support for these people by adopting a drag persona in the video for his track Yo Perreo Sola. And, following the release of this video, the praises for this straight man skyrocketed, which also made him receive outsized attention for his LGBTQ activism. At the same time, this even pulled Bunny to have sexuality rumors as some thought him to be gay.

In fact, the actor is straight and has a girlfriend Gabriela Berlingeri and they made their red carpet debut as a couple in 2021.

El Muerto Star Bad Bunny Drag Makeover & Trans Rumors

Bad Bunny exhibited no reluctance and got a full drag makeover for the track Yo Perreo Sola as mentioned in Rolling Stone.

Making his drag debut to show support to trans people or those who need it, his first exposure to the drag world started from his family members. Well, his cousin, who is a drag performer, ca,e to help to ask for his help in making mixes for shows.

After the release of Yo Perreo Sola, Benito told that even though he may not be gay, he is a human who cares. Thus, he made a huge and inspiring move and thus portrayed himself in a drag makeover. Bad Bunny also revealed that he has felt that there was a part of him that made him feel like he had a feminine side. But, his drag makeover in the video cleared his doubts as he felt that he, in fact, has a masculine side.

Bad Bunny uses his platform to speak out openly about sexuality, gender, dressing up in drag, etc. Actually, in one of his music videos, he has even noted the sensitive issue of gender-based violence, to boost awareness about it among his song listeners.

Bad Bunny Gender Bending Characters And Songs Explored

Bad Bunny has been referred to as a gender-bending rapper who portrays such characters in his songs and videos.

As a matter of fact, Bad Bunny has never shied away from continuing to break gender norms, expressing gender fluidity, and pushing gender boundaries. Well, one time, he even wore a short pink dress.
