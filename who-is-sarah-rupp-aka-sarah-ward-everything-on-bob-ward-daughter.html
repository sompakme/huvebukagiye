Who Is Sarah Rupp, AKA Sarah Ward?

Sarah Rupp is prominent as the daughter of Bob Ward.

But, besides that, she is the owner of the flyover Farm, a farm for horses.

Sarah has been a trainer at numerous farms in the Southeast, including running large school programs at various facilities. She has also worked as a barn manager, head groom, exercise rider, saddle expert, and vet-tech, giving her a well-rounded equine resume.​

She offers both group and private lessons tailored to the individual needs of the horse and rider.
