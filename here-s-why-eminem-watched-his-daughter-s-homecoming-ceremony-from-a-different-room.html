Here's Why Eminem Watched His Daughter's Homecoming Ceremony From A Different Room

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  541 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Rap star Eminem reportedly attended his daughter’s homecoming ceremony to see Hailie Jade Scott Mathers crowned homecoming queen of her Michigan high school back in 2013. However, the rapper watched in private in a different room, and here’s the reason why.

Why Did Eminem Watch Hailie’s Coronation in Private?

Hailie Jade, who was only 17-years-old at that time, was hailed as the homecoming queen of Chippewa Valley High School on October 4, 2013 in Clinton Township, Michigan. Students and faculty chose the beauty, who was described by those who know her as a “smart, athletic and caring young woman,” as the queen after a vote.

With her success at a young age as being chosen the queen at her school, her parents were surely proud of her. A parent who was not named revealed to the news outlet, “Hailie came out with her mother Kim when she was introduced with all other kids but her father watched from inside the school because he didn’t want to cause a scene – he wanted to have her own moment.”

Another parent said the American rapper was beaming when his daughter’s name was called. “He opened the door and looked out like – ‘That’s my daughter!’ He just looked like a proud father,” the parent recounted. Eminem, who admits that his daughter Hailie serves as his primary source of motivation, did not take the spotlight from her and instead, watched the proceedings privately in a different room.

Not only is the hip-hop icon a proud father during the homecoming ceremony, but he also very personal when it comes to his songs. He often includes references about his mother, ex-wife, and most famously, his daughter Hailie. He mentions his daughter more than 23 times in the songs including, Beautiful, My Darling, and Hailie’s Song.

Where Is Hailie Now And What Does She Do?

The 25-year-old beauty has accomplished a lot in her life so far. She graduated Summa Cum Laude in high school in 2014. She then earned a degree in psychology. She admits that the encouragement of her influential father play a big part in her continued success. In her high school newsletter, she reportedly said, “My mother and father because they have pushed me to be the person I am and have given me all the support to achieve what I have.

Now she’s a famous Instagram influencer with 2.1 million followers. When it comes to her posts in social media, she has been posting positivity to lighten the day of her followers. In a photo she shared, she captioned it with: “Hi here’s a friendly reminder that you have the power to make today a good day.” She also impresses her fans with her fashion sense, which she enjoys expressing herself through her clothing.

She never runs out of thoughts to feed the social media platform with positivity. These little acts of kindness from an influencer can definitely go a long way in helping someone else’s day. Given her reach, it is a great use of her social media power.
