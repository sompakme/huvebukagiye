Chris Pratt almost lost his life while on a vacation, but his ex-girlfriend, who was good with horses, saved his life. Chris Pratt has made several blockbuster films, including The Lego Movie, Avengers: Endgame and one of his best films; Jurassic World. Surprisingly, none of these big films required him to ride a horse until The Magnificent Seven came along. Pratt said before filming that movie he had bad luck with horses.

One of the most horrible experiences he had with horses came when he tried to show off his riding prowess to a girl he was dating. “I actually had to get rescued one time by the girl I was dating. We were on horses on this beach, and it was supposed to be romantic.” However, the experience was far from romantic because of Pratt's cockiness, and it also ruined his relationship. In this article, we will examine what happened when Pratt went horse riding with his ex-girlfriend and his romantic relationships after that experience.

Chris Pratt Almost Died While On Vacation With His Ex-Girlfriend

While on Late Night With Seth Meyers, to promote his latest project, the Guardians of the Galaxy actor revealed he almost died while on vacation while riding a horse. Pratt explained he had gotten cocky about his horse riding skills, and it almost led to his death. He told Meyers he had always had a contentious relationship with horses growing up because he treated them like ‘jet-skis’ instead of living animals.

"I didn't fully put together that it was a living animal, I would just jump... and also my confidence would quickly outgrow my skill."

Before he began dating Anna Faris, Pratt had an ex-girlfriend who loved horses. Once, they decided to go on a romantic vacation and ride horses on the beach. Because he was so cocky about his skills, Pratt ended up in a horrible situation when the horse he was riding took off running, and his feet came up on the stirrups.

"My feet were up in the air, my head was bouncing off the horse's ass, I was seconds away from death."
