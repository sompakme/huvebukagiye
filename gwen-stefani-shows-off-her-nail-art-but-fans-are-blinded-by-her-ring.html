Gwen Stefani Shows Off Her Nail Art, But Fans Are Blinded By Her Ring

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Everything about Gwen Stefani has fans not only interested, but downright addicted. Her happiness is infectious, and her smile is contagious, so it's no wonder that millions of people eagerly await updates to her social media posts. She's dropping new music at a furious pace, and fans just can't help but stare at her seemingly ageless image as she takes the world by storm with videos and images that keep her fans regularly engaged.

Her most recent post is one that fans were quick to gravitate towards. Clearly impressed with her newest nail art, Gwen posted a fabulous image of her intricately detailed nails... but fans were so buys gawking at her massive ring that they found it hard to focus on the manicure!

Gwen's Massive Ring

Go ahead, stare at it. Every one of us has done the same. There are no words to describe the flawless masterpiece that sits on Gwen's finger, other than to say, it's absolutely stunning, and ridiculously huge. It's hard to imagine that this ring is real, as its size and shape are so immaculate, and unlike anything the average person could ever afford.

Blake Shelton is said to have dropped $500,000 on this masterpiece, and it definitely shows. Carrying the value of the average person's house on her tiny little finger, Gwen Stefani's Instagram post depicts some pretty fancy nails to match the bling.

If you can look past the ring long enough to notice her nails, you'll see Stefani had dedicated one of them to the man that put that fancy shiny rock on her finger.

An Ode To Blake Shelton

The last image in her post reveals that Gwen Stefani's bubble gum pink nails have been specially dedicated to the love of her life, Blake Shelton.

The rest of her nails are finely detailed with butterflies and rhinestones, adding to the very feminine look, but her pinky makes an appearance in the last photograph, and is adorably dedicated to Blake. His name appears written in black, just above an image of cupid on top of a heart. This very cute touch makes this already perfect Instagram post even better.

Fans could barely notice the nail art at first, as the ring completely stole the thunder.
