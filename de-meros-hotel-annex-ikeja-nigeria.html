De Meros Hotel Annex, Ikeja, Nigeria

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  454 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

De Meros Hotel & Suites Annex offers unique hospitality services and shopping experience to all cadres of guest within the comfort of its elegant architectural. This top class hotel welcomes guest to a world of hospitality, luxury and comfort design at 9/11 CMD Road, G.R.A, Ikosi Magodo Phase 2. It affords guests with easy access within and outside the cosmopolitan city of Lagos.

The tastefully furnished air conditioned rooms are fully equipped with en-suite toilet and bathrooms with towels and toiletries, dressing mirrors, closet, hair dryer, intercom, wall paintings, wall lamps, kettles, study desk and chair, sitting area with sofas, television with cable channels and refrigerators. There is free Wi-Fi access available throughout the premises. Rooms at this hotel are grouped into Standard, Deluxe, Executive Suite, Royal Suite, Royal Executive Suite and Presidential Suite.

Guests can order a variety of meals and drinks at the bar. The swimming pool is a major recreation area where guests can relax and also have access to the pool bar. A conference hall/meeting room is also available at the hotel for social and corporate events.

Other services rendered by De Meros Hotel Annex include uninterrupted power supply, room service, adequate security and parking. Laundry service, car hire and business centre can be provided to guests on request at a charged fee.
