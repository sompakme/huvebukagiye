Vernon Hargreaves III College

Vernon Hargreaves accepted an athletic scholarship to attend the University of Florida, where he has played for head coaches Will Muschamp and Jim McElwain’s Florida Gators football teams in Southeastern Conference (SEC) competition in 2013, 2014, and 2015.

As a sophomore, Vernon Hargreaves played 12 games with three interceptions, 13 passes defended, and two fumble recoveries on 50 tackles.

Vernon Hargreaves was selected by The Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the first round (11th overall) of the 2016 NFL Draft and he was the third cornerback to be selected.
