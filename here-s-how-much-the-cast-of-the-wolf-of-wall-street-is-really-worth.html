Heres How Much The Cast Of The Wolf Of Wall Street Is Really Worth

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1271 words  |  Aldo Pusey

During Martin Scorsese’s legendary career, the Hollywood heavyweight has directed several of the most acclaimed movies of all time. For that reason, Scorsese was able to accumulate an incredibly star-studded cast, to say the very least, when he began production on The Wolf of Wall Street.

As anyone who has seen The Wolf of Wall Street will already know, the movie’s plot revolved around the accumulation of money through any means. Even though The Wolf of Wall Street was meant to send the message of the dangers of greed, the fact remains that the film’s stars have loads of money at their disposal. With that in mind, it begs an obvious question, which The Wolf of Wall Street star is the richest.

12 Jordan Belfort – Net Worth: $-100 Million

Since The Wolf of Wall Street revolves around Leonardo DiCaprio bringing Jordan Belfort to life, the movie’s many fans are very familiar with the former stockbroker’s corrupt past. However, one thing that a lot of The Wolf of Wall Street don’t know is that the real Belfort appeared in one of the film’s scenes as an Auckland Straight Line host. As a result of his crimes, Belfort was ordered to pay $110 million in restation to his victims. Thanks to the money Belfort makes as a motivational speaker, he has managed to pay some of that off but he is still $100 million in debt.

11 Cristin Milioti – Net Worth: $4 Million

Over the last several years, Cristin Milioti has become one of the most in-demand actors around since she is great in every role. As a result, Milioti has landed roles in shows like How I Met Your Mother, Fargo, Black Mirror, and Made for Love on top of movies like The Wolf of Wall Street and Palm Springs. Thanks to the success she has enjoyed, Milioti has accumulated a $4 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

10 Ethan Suplee – Net Worth: $8 Million

Throughout Ethan Suplee’s career, he has appeared in a lot of different movies and TV shows. Still, his greatest claims to fame are appearing in several Kevin Smith movies and starring in American History X and Remember the Titans on top of shows like My Name Is Earl and Boy Meets World. Thanks to being an acting jack of all trades, Suplee has an $8 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

9 Jon Bernthal - $10 Million

After Jon Bernthal was cast as The Walking Dead’s Shane Walsh, millions of fans became aware of how much charisma the actor has. As a result, Bernthal has landed a lot of roles since his Walking Dead days ended. Most notably, Bernthal joined the Marvel Cinematic Universe when he was cast Frank Castle and starred in Daredevil and then went on to play the same character in his own show, The Punisher. Even though it still seems like the best days of Bernthal’s career may still be ahead for him, he already is worth $10 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

8 Kyle Chandler – Net Worth: $12 Million

Throughout Kyle Chandler’s career, he has appeared in many movies including The Wolf of Wall Street, Godzilla: King of the Monsters, and Godzilla vs. Kong. Despite those roles, Chandler is best known as television’s secret weapon due to starring in shows like Friday Night Lights and Bloodline on top of his recurring Grey's Anatomy role. Primarily thanks to Chandler’s television success, he is worth $12 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

7 Jean Dujardin – Net Worth: $16 Million

An extremely talented French actor, Jean Dujardin initially became well known in his home country. Then Dujardin rose to international fame after he won a Best Actor Oscar for his starring role in The Artist, a movie that also won the Academy Award for Best Picture. Following that breakthrough, Dujardin went on to appear in American movies like The Wolf of Wall Street and The Monuments Men. As a result of his highly successful career, Dujardin is worth $16 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

6 Margot Robbie – Net Worth: $26 Million

In addition to hiring many major stars to headline The Wolf of Wall Street, Martin Scorsese also cast a much less known actor named Margot Robbie for one of the movie’s biggest roles. Thanks to her head-turning performance in The Wolf of Wall Street, Robbie has gone on to star in many huge movies. Robbie’s most notable roles to date include playing Harley Quinn in several comic book movies, and starring in films like Once Upon a Time in Hollywood and I, Tonya. As a result of taking Hollywood by storm, Robbie is now worth $26 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

5 Jonah Hill – Net Worth: $50 Million

When Jonah Hill first rose to fame, most people thought of him as solely a comedic actor. As time went on, however, Hill starred in The Wolf of Wall Street and that played a role in people coming to realize he can do it all in Hollywood. As a result of starring in movies of every genre and becoming a producer, writer, and director, Hill is now worth $50 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

4 Jon Favreau – Net Worth: $100 Million

Despite first breaking through as the writer and star of the independent film Swingers, Jon Favreau has become one of the most impactful people in Hollywood. After all, Favreau directed the first Marvel Cinematic Universe film Iron Man, he helmed 2019’s The Lion King, and he created The Mandalorian and The Book of Boba Fett. Favreau also is an accomplished actor which has added to his ability to build a $100 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

3 Matthew McConaughey – Net Worth: $140 Million

After the success of movies like Dazed and Confused and A Time to Kill, Matthew McConaughey became one of the most talked-about young actors in Hollywood. Sadly, however, after several years of relevance, McConaughey’s career hit the skids and he appeared in many quickly forgotten films. Then, McConaughey’s performances in Dallas Buyers Club and The Wolf of Wall Street rejuvenated his career and now he is worth $140 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

2 Rob Reiner – Net Worth: $200 Million

Back in 1971, Rob Reiner began starring in one of the most celebrated sitcoms of all time, All in the Family. Even though most people would rest on their laurels after landing a role like that, Reiner went on to become one of the most successful directors in Hollywood. After all, Reiner helmed movies like This Is Spinal Tap, Stand by Me, The Princess Bride, When Harry Met Sally…, Misery, A Few Good Men, and many more. Due to his hallowed place in Hollywood, Reiner is currently worth $200 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

1 Leonardo DiCaprio – Net Worth: $260 Million

Even in a list of extremely wealthy movie stars, there is always going to be one who has more money than all the rest. When it comes to the stars of The Wolf of Wall Street, it is Leonardo DiCaprio who is the wealthiest. Given the fact that DiCaprio starred in some extremely successful films, including Titanic, Inception, The Departed, The Revenant, and The Wolf of Wall Street, that makes perfect sense. As a result of those roles, DiCaprio is currently worth $260 million according to celebritynetworth.com.
