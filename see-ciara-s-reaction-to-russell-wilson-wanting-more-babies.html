See Ciaras Reaction to Russell Wilson Wanting More Babies

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  426 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Ready for more? Ciara hilariously shot down her husband, Russell Wilson, after he hinted the couple should have another baby — less than two months after their son Win’s birth.

“So cute. He’s so precious,” the “Goodies” singer, 34, said in an Instagram video posted to Wilson’s account on Wednesday, September 2. “Win, hi baby.”

The Seattle Seahawks quarterback, 31, captioned the clip, “’Sir Win!’ Late Nights! Team No Sleep! 😂😍😂 .”

Throughout the video, Ciara, who is offscreen, gushed over her newest bundle of joy as Wilson held onto the little one, who is dressed for bed in star pajamas.

“Say, ‘What’s up mommy? You gonna feed me? That’s what’s up,’” the football player said before hinting that the baby shouldn’t be their last.

He looked at his wife and added, “We need to have more of these little ones,” to which the musician replied, “Okay, sit down!” before ending the video.

The “Paint It, Black” singer, who gave birth to her third child (Wilson’s second) on July 23, revealed last month that she is currently focused on a new fitness goal following Win’s arrival.

“48lbs to go! Starting the game plan tomorrow!!” she wrote via Instagram in August. “P.s. don’t know how easy it’s going be considering 3 [babies] now! Going to work really hard at this! Let’s go Mamma’s .”

Ciara shares 3-year-old daughter Sienna with the athlete and 6-year-old son Future Jr. with her ex Future. Since welcoming another little one to her squad, the Texas native has stayed busy, releasing new music in between raising her kids.

In August, the “1, 2 Step” artist dropped the music video for “Rooted,” which she filmed while pregnant and finished two days before going into labor.

The video, which also stars her two other kids, was dropped the same day as the track. The mother of three released the song with a goal of donating a portion of the proceeds to Grantmakers for Girls of Color.
