The Skinnyshirt Concept and Design

Skinnyshirt is a unique product that combines the design of a button-down shirt with a stretchy material collar and buttons. It aims to provide the appearance and feel of a button-down shirt worn under a sweater or jacket, but without the wrinkles and bunching that can occur with regular button-down shirts.

This innovative design features a collar made from stretchy material, allowing it to fit snugly and comfortably around the neck. The stretchy buttons provide flexibility and prevent pulling on the collar, ensuring a sleek and polished look.

Secondly, manufacturing difficulties were another factor that led to the rejection. The Sharks were worried about the feasibility of scaling up production and ensuring consistent quality. These concerns highlighted potential risks and challenges in the manufacturing process that could impact the business’s long-term success.

Lastly, there were doubts about Julie Kalimian’s management abilities and long-term strategy. The Sharks questioned whether she had the necessary experience and skills to navigate the competitive fashion industry and effectively lead the company to success.

Despite the rejection on Shark Tank, Julie Kalimian remained determined and continued to pursue Skinnyshirt outside of the show. However, it is essential to note that this rejection played a significant role in the company’s journey and eventual closure in 2018.
